Description

An exceptional example of the iconic 1969 Speedmaster Professional, reference 145.022-69. This 'Pre-Moon' model features the renowned caliber 861 movement, transitioning from the earlier 321 while retaining the classic stepped dial and applied logo aesthetic that collectors covet.

The matte black dial has aged beautifully, exhibiting a warm, even patina on the tritium hour markers and hands. The 'Dot Over 90' (DON) bezel is in remarkable condition, showing only minor wear commensurate with its age, adding to the watch's authentic vintage character.

Housed in a 42mm stainless steel asymmetrical case, the watch has been carefully preserved. It has recently undergone a sympathetic service by our master watchmaker to ensure timekeeping accuracy while meticulously preserving all original components and finishes. The watch is presented on a high-quality vintage-style leather strap.
